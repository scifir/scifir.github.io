Scifir

Hello! Scifir is a digital foundation committed to improve science. At the present, it's improving technologies for personal laboratories in order to ammeliorate independent research, as well as publishing articles about scientific topics, centrally creational biology, which is a branch of biology created by Ismael Correa Castro, the founder of Scifir.

Scifir is a digital foundation, which means it's a foundation that operates over the web, and not inside any specific country.
