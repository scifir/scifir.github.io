Requisites

  • Know english and/or spanish. If you know both, better!
  • Optionally, to know russian.
  • To know programming, if a good amount better!

Important

It's not necessary to know a lot about programming or of one of the three natural sciences, physics, chemistry or biology. If you're interested in contributing to science, and you are willing to do some effort for that, you can contribute! There are tasks to do for different levels of knowledge about science.
