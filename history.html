History of Scifir

Scifir has been founded by Ismael Correa Castro in 2023 to publish all his scientific projects which he started in 2007.

Our timeline

  • 2007: The DNA Editor is invented, along with creational biology.
  • 2007: The L3D file format is invented. Published in 2023.
  • 2007: The DWML file format gets designed.
  • 2007: The CWD file format gets designed. Published in 2023.
  • 2007: The role materializer gets designed. Published in 2023.
  • 2017-2018: The scifir collection is developed during two years. A wide majority of it gets developed. The C++ libraries developed are scifir-units, scifir-geometry, scifir-chemistry and scifir-biology.
