About Scifir

Scifir, acronym of "Science fiction to reality", is a digital foundation founded by Ismael Correa Castro in 2023. It has been created with the necessity of the Ismael to publish all his scientific inventions, theories and articles, which at 2023 were a vast amount mandatory to be published somewhere. Some inventions, like the DNA Editor, are considered by Ismael to be a very important technology.


A digital foundation is how is called here a foundation that operates only by digital means, and not inside any building of the material world. Also, Scifir operates inside internet, outside any country.


Scifir works under open science for all his projects. Open science is a movement that operates under six principles: open methodology, open source, open data, open access, open peer review, open educational resources.
